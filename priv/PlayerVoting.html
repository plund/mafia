Mafia Player Voting

Player Vote Meaning
##VOTE (player_name) During the day, this is how to vote to lynch a player
##VOTE NO-LYNCH During the day, this is how to vote to not lynch any player.
It is accepted to end a day this way.
##UNVOTE
##UNVOTE (player_name) 		This removes your vote to lynch a player. The (player_name) is not needed, but can be added to make clear to everyone who you unvote if you want to point that out.
It is accepted to end a day with an unvote (in Jamies game it was not).
##END
##VOTE END 		Vote for ending this phase early, when voting for a player.
##UNEND
##VOTE UNEND 		Remove your vote for ending this phase early. Also an ##unvote and a ##vote change to another player will remove your ##END vote.

Notes:

Upper or lower case does not matter. The bots converts all text to upper case before it starts looking for player votes.

The player that at the end of day have received more votes than any other player and more votes than the No-Lynch alternative, is lynched (dies) by the end of the day. The number of Unvote votes does not count.
