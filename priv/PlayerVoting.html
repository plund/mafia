Mafia Player Voting

Player Vote Effect on the Mafia tracker bot
##VOTE (player_name) During the day, this is how to vote to lynch a player
##VOTE NO-LYNCH During the day, this is how to vote to not lynch any player.
It is accepted to end a day this way.
##UNVOTE
##UNVOTE (player_name) 		This removes your vote to lynch a player. The (player_name) is not needed, but can be added to make clear to everyone who you unvote if you want to point that out.
It is NOT accepted to end a day with an unvote.
##END Vote for ending this phase early.
##UNEND Remove your vote for ending this phase early.

Notes:

Upper or lower case does not matter. The bots converts all text to upper case before it starts looking for GM commands.
